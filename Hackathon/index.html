What is Dopamine Clicker?

Dopmaine Clicker is a game similar to cookie clicker where you click on the big button on your screen to earn dopamine.
You can then use that dopamine to buy powerups, which will in turn give you even more dopamine! Check out a list of powerups and how to get them here.

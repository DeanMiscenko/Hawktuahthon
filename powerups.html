Powerups & Upgrades

Upgrades

  • Click Multiplier - Doubles the amount of dopamine earned per click.
  • Powerup Booster - Doubles the dopamine earned from a specific powerup (separate upgrade for each powerup).
  • Critical Click - 5% chance for a click to generate 5x the normal dopamine.

Powerups

  • Shooting Stars - Stars fly across the screen, rewarding bonus dopamine when clicked.
  • Spinning Wheel - A wheel appears with a chance to hit a jackpot for massive dopamine rewards.
  • Subway Surfers Gameplay - A mini Subway Surfers game plays in the corner, providing dopamine bonuses.
  • Rainbow Cursor - Your cursor changes colors as you click, increasing dopamine gain.
  • Lo-Fi Study Music - Relaxing background music plays, boosting dopamine per second (DPS).
  • Color Gradient - The background shifts colors dynamically, making clicks feel more satisfying.
  • Screen Zoom Effect - The screen zooms in and out with each click, enhancing dopamine impact.
